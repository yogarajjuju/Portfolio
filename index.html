About Me
Who I
Am.

I'm Yogaraj S — a final-year B.Tech student in AI & Data Science at VSB College of Engineering, Coimbatore. My real education happened in the terminal.

I got into cybersecurity through curiosity — wanting to know not just how systems work, but how they break. That led to pentesting, vulnerability research, and bug bounty hunting. I've published a patent on integrating Snort with Darktrace for cyber defense, and co-authored work on blockchain-based e-voting.

On the DevOps & systems side, I daily-drive Hyprland on Fedora, maintain open source dotfiles, build Android apps in Kotlin, and write bots for fun. I care about automation, clean tooling, and software that is as secure as it is elegant.

Actively seeking roles in security engineering, DevOps, or software development.
